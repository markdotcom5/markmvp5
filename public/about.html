About SharedStars

Making Space Accessible for Everyone

Our Mission

Our mission is to democratize space travel by equipping individuals with the knowledge, certifications, AR/VR experiences, and our comprehensive 10‑module program.

SharedStars transforms space travel dreams into reality by building an inclusive community of Subscribing Space Travelers (SSTs) who are united in shaping the future of space exploration.

Our Vision

To create a world where space travel is inclusive and attainable for all—fostering a global community united by the aspiration to explore beyond Earth.

My Journey

I started SharedStars with a vision to empower individuals with the tools and technology to reach for the stars. Combining years of expertise in tech startups, AI, and aerospace, I embarked on a mission to make space travel accessible for everyone. My journey wasn’t easy—it was built on relentless innovation, overcoming challenges, and a steadfast commitment to transforming dreams into reality.

Today, our community continues to grow and evolve, driven by passion, innovation, and the belief that the future of space is for all of us.
