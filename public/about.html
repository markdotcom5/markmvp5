About SharedStars

Making Space Accessible for Everyone

Our Mission

Our mission is to democratize space travel by equipping individuals with the knowledge, certifications, AR/VR experiences, classroom training, and our comprehensive 10-module program.

SharedStars transforms space travel dreams into reality by creating an inclusive and vibrant community of Subscribing Space Travelers (SSTs) who are united in shaping the future of space exploration.

Our Vision

To create a world where space travel is inclusive and attainable for all, fostering a global community united by the aspiration to explore beyond Earth.
