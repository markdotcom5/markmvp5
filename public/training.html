Training Modules

Explore various modules to enhance your physical, mental, and technical readiness for space exploration.

Mental

  • Solve puzzles, memory games, or mental agility tasks.
  • Earn credits for completing scenario-based problem-solving simulations.

Psychological

  • Complete relaxation or mindfulness exercises.
  • Maintain journaling or emotional self-reflection streaks.
  • Participate in group empathy activities.

Spiritual

  • Complete guided meditations.
